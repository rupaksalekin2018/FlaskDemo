About Our Project

In a country like Bangladesh, our economy is greatly dependent on agriculture. Among all the crops rice production plays vital role in the economy. Although many problems occur during production but due to inability to recognize plant diseases, plenty of our crops are being lost every year. The cause of the diseases are mainly bacteria and virus and can occur in both nursery and main field stage. Diseases include blast, brown spot and sheath rot etc. The loss of crops introduces food shortage, sometimes use of unsupervised of excessive pesticides which affects the economy and the environment. But there is hardly any effective system available for the farmers and the farms to recognise the disease at an early stage. That is why we are trying to fill up the gap as much as possible. As a result we have created a system where an user can know about diseases of his/her plant is affected. For that user has to use a smartphone or camera to click a picture of affected plants and upload that on our website. After diagnosis, they will get an accurate result and will know the name of disease of the plant/crops.
