Impact of Our Project

The most common plant diseases are caused by virus, bacteria and insects. A large number of crops are lost every year because of affecting plant diseases. Our project can detect plant diseases at an early stage. Key impacts of our project are:

  • Decrease of crop loss through early detection.
  • Prevention of food shortage.
  • Increase of supply through increased production.
